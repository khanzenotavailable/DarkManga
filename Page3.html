Horror heavyweights Junji Ito and Hirokatsu Kihara are teaming up again for nine short and terrifying stories.

Hirokatsu Kihara weaves together authentic tales of mysteries left unsolved, enhanced by a captivating array of original illustrations from Junji Ito. Within this collection lie nine chilling narratives, accompanied by a bonus manga story, creating a compelling fusion of reality and the supernatural.

'Stitches' by Hirokatsu Kihara releases Spring 2024.
