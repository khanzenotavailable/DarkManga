Manga vs. Anime

Manga and anime both often use similar distinctive illustration styles, but the word manga specifically refers to Japanese-style graphic novels (book-length comic books), while anime refers to the genre of Japanese-style 2D animation. In other words, manga are books, while anime consists of shows and movies.
